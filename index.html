VPC5 Round 1:

A Meshing of Style

Overall Theme:

Each vocalist and producer has their own sound and their own story, this challenge is about the individual and the team. Tell the story of the vocalist, the producer, and then mesh the two together to tell the story of the team. Maybe your producer has a story about that time he saved 22 puppies from Vladimir Putin in a hot air balloon – or that time your vocalist had to run for his life after faking the funk on a nasty dunk. We don’t know – so you will have to tell us. This round, it’s all about your team.

Oh, and vocalists, you don't get to write a hook.

Enjoy.

Vocalist Challenge:

You’ve got three verses to tell us the story of you, your partner and your team. Your past, your present and your future aspirations all need to come through in a comprehensive and digestible tale that may or may not be based in reality. You are free to write your own mythos. You are a storyteller, and like all stories we expect a beginning, a middle and an end.

Guidelines:

  1. This is loose so long as you write a track that tells us a story with you and your producer as the main characters.
  2. You may not assume any new or alternative persona. These need to remain the persona of you and your producer. But can be set anywhere in or outside of reality.
  3. Avoid being a basic bitch.

Producer Challenge:

Your beat needs to do three things, and none of them are going to be easy:

Guidelines:

  1. The beat needs to be a banger but also be intentionally minimal during the verses – there needs (needs: cannot avoid – must) to be a focus on your vocalist during the verses
  2. You must compose an entirely original instrumental piece for the hook. You may chop, screw and sample your vocalist but the cuts must be distorted to such a point that they are not coherent lyrics. You will be judged very harshly should you choose to sample your vocalist.
  3. A bridge, breakdown or interlude that is reflective of your hook must be incorporated into your piece and be prominently displayed. Don’t outshine your vocalist.
